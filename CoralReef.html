The Animals That Live There

Coral reefs, the beautiful underwater world full of color and life. The animals that live there do not
disapoint.


Buffy the Butterflyfish

Butterflyfish typically live in the warm, shallow waters of the Pacific and Indian oceans. Occasionally, you may
also find them in the Atlantic Ocean. Buffy is a beautiful fish and certaintyly a brave one. All of our staff love
her.

Stager the Staghorn coral

The staghorn coral is a branching, stony coral with cylindrical branches ranging from a few centimetres
to over two metres in length and height. Although Stager is not a fish, he is still very much alive. He helps a lot
of people realize that plants are creatures and just as interesting as every other animal in our zoo.
