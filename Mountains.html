The Mountain Habitat

The Animals That Live There

The mountains, home to many interesting yet mystical animals. What to us seems like rocky terrain and tough conditions is home to some of our lovely animals here at the zoo. Although we don't have them all, we have do have some friends here that originated for this lovely habitat.


Willson the Wolf

The wolf, also known as the gray wolf or grey wolf, is a large canine native to Eurasia and North America.
Willson was found injured by one of our workers who was out exploring. Our team came to its rescue and Willson is now
happily recovering in our lovely zoo.

George the Mountain Goat

The mountain goat, also known as the Rocky Mountain goat, is a cloven-footed mammal that is
endemic to the remote and rugged mountainous areas of western North America.George was found motherless and fatherless by one of
our teams and taken in since he wouldn't be able to survive on his own.
