The Desert Habitat

The Animals That Live There

Deserts are known for their dryness and their high temperatures. But they should be more well known for all the amazing animals that
this interesting habitat houses.


Fenix the Fennec Fox

Fennec Fox - The fennec fox is a common desert animal that lives in the Sahara desert. They eat insects, small rodents,
lizards, birds, and eggs. Fenix was brought to us by another zoo who couldn't house him so now he stays with us, and we couldn't
be happier about it.

Miku the Meerkat

The meerkat or suricate is a small mongoose found in southern Africa. It is characterised by a broad head,
large eyes, a pointed snout, long legs, a thin tapering tail, and a brindled coat pattern. Miku came to us under
the same circumstances as Fenix, and she's a crowd favorite.
