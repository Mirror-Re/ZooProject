The Arctic Habitat

The Animals That Live There

The arctic is known for its freezing temperatures and barren land. But there are animals that make this
seemingly unsurvivable habitat their life-long home.


Ash the Arctic Hare

The Arctic hare is a species of hare highly adapted to living in the Arctic tundra and other icy biomes. Ash was found
by another zoo that didn't have the resources to care for her. We took her in and she adapted quickly. We plan to release
her in a few months, but for now she's enjoying her stay here.

Walden the Walrus

The walrus is a large pinniped marine mammal with discontinuous distribution about the North Pole in the
Arctic Ocean and subarctic seas of the Northern Hemisphere. Walden was rescued by our team a few months ago and
we took him in while he heals up. We have plans to release him back at his home soon.
